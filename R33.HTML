1. Nissan Skyline GTR R33

The 1996 Nissan Skyline GT-R R33 was constructed by Newera Imports. The Skyline was originally bought unmodified and with low mileage. It was later modified in California for use in Tokyo Drift.

In the Hotwheels Variation, the car has rubber tires, gray and blue color variations, decorative details in which replicates the real car.
