Nissan Skyline GTR R34

The Nissan Skylines created for the film were all R34s (the model was debuted in 1998). The R34s in the movie were powered by an RB26DETT 2.6-liter, DOHC, 24-valve straight six engine located longitudinally in the engine compartment, force fed by twin turbos, and officially rated at 280 horsepower. The R34's engine powers a six-speed Getrag gearbox, which transmits power to the ATTESA all-wheel-drive system, which automatically adjusts the torque split.

In the Hotwheels Variation, the car has rubber tires, midnight blue and gray stripping details, decorative details in which replicates the real car.
